How it works

Three, alarmingly simple, steps.

Questions? No problem

Check out our FAQs

Step 1 – Get Ready
Answer a bunch of super simple questions – using up about 3 minutes of your day – to understand if your home is ready for a heat pump. If it is, we’ll give you some useful info to see if you want to keep going.
Step 2 – Get Steady
Or rather, make sure you’re sure. Go into more detail – whilst in the comfort of your own home – and provide some further details to make things more accurate. Add: 30 minutes. 40 if you’re drinking tea at the same time.
Step 3 – Go!
Get a home survey. Get a final price from local trusted and capable contractors. Access the required finance, if you need it. Sign on the dotted line – only if you’re still happy. Press on with whatever it is you’ve decided to do. Marvellous.
