What is Thermly?
Here at Thermly, we unscramble a confusing and disorganised experience and give you simple information to make informed decisions that are tailored to your home. We then match you with accredited installers and give you the power to manage the process.
Personalised

We combine a whole bunch of data about housing with what you share with us to create advice tailored specifically to you.

Connected

We connect you with talented and capable installers able to undertake the work to the highest standard.

Supportive

Everything can be managed through Thermly, making it easy for everyone, so you can rest assured we’ve always got your back.

How it works
Three, extremely simple, steps.
Get Ready.
Answer a bunch of super easy questions – taking up about 5 minutes of your day. If your home is ready for a heat pump, we'll instantly tell you, and we'll also tell you why.
Get Set...
Keen to keep going? Great, we just need a bit more information to make things more accurate. It'll take about 30 more minutes of your time. 40 if you're drinking tea.
Get going!
Simply use Thermly to select your preferred, trusted and capable local installer and book a survey. After that, you can access the required finance (if you need it) and book an installation. Marvellous.
