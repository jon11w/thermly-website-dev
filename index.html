How it works

Three, extremely simple, steps.

Got a question?

No worries, click here for our FAQs

Step 1: Get Ready.
We need you to take a readiness assessment. Simply, answer a bunch of super easy questions – taking up about 5 minutes of your day – to understand if your home is ready for a heat pump. If it is, we'll give you some useful information on costs and benefits to see if you want to keep going.

If we don’t think your home is ready, we'll tell you - and then we'll tell you exactly why and how Thermly could still help. We don't abandon you that easily!
Step 2: Get Set...
OK, so you want more information? Whilst in the comfort of your own home? Great - stay right where you are. We just need a bit more detail to make things more accurate. It'll take about 30 more minutes of your time. 40 if you're drinking tea.

After this, we'll give you some clear next steps - and generate an information pack which your local installer can use to cost up a quote quickly and with confidence.
Step 3: Get going!
Now things are getting real. It's time to get a home survey. Simply select your preferred, trusted and capable local installer using our Home Intelligence Platform and book a survey using the Thermly booking system.

After that, you can access the required finance (if you need it), book an installation - and start enjoying a life with sustainable energy. Marvellous.
