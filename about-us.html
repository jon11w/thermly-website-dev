Our values
At all times, we put you at the center of the process.
Customer-led

We'll be honest, we will do everything we can for you, but this doesn’t necessarily mean you'll always be right! But you must always be heard and supported. We promise to do all these things: listen, react quickly and do the right thing at all times. And advise you when we think you could be doing something a little differently.

Open and honest

We will only promise what we can actually deliver. That's why we're starting small, focusing on heat pumps and in just a couple of locations, before expanding. We'll be transparent about everything. For example, we won't promise you energy savings if we don’t think there are any for you. If we don’t think you should spend the money, we'll tell you.

Independent

We are not linked to anyone else, and we like it that way because you then get the impartial advice and support you need.

Good business

Yes, we're a commercial company but that doesn't mean we'll conduct business that does harm. We're working towards B-Corp status to show we meet the highest standards of social and environmental performance, transparency and accountability. We're committed to positively impacting all stakeholders – workers, customers, communities and our planet - and will do everything we can to achieve this commitment.
