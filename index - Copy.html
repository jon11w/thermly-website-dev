Why Thermly?
Here's why we exist.

We set up Thermly because the experience of installing green technologies in the home was pretty dreadful - and it could be done a lot better. Here at Thermly, we unscramble a confusing and disorganised experience and give you simple information to make informed decisions that are tailored to your home. We then match you with accredited installers. Easy.

You are vital

Yes, we use lots of data and funky analytics, but you are central to the process. You know your home better than anyone, so it's useful to get the inside track from you too. This not only helps us help you, but it means you are always in control.

It's quick

It really is, and we will save you so much time too. Oh, and it's intuitive. And very easy to use. We could go on. We think this makes quite a tedious exercise much more enjoyable.

We're independent

We're not linked to any particular manufacturer or installer. And we like it that way - because you then get the impartial advice and support you need.
