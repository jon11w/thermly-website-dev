Why should you trust Thermly with your heat pump installation?

Buying advice

Hi! We’re Thermly. We help householders make informed decisions about whether, when and how to install a heat pump - making their homes more energy efficient.

Let’s face it, the energy industry can be bewildering. We know we operate in a confused, fragmented and, dare we say it, dry industry. We don’t want to be any of these things. We want to buck the trend - and we want to bring a little joy to the process of finding the right heat source for you.

Why we are who we are.

We’re here because we realised there’s a better way of doing things. The tools people have available to research heat pumps and other sustainable energy solutions are often laborious, complex and confusing.

We make this more straightforward. With us, you will be able to easily find out if your home is suitable for a heat pump, secure Government grants of up to £7,500 to help with the upfront cost and accelerate the installation process by matching you with a local, trusted and certified installer who will do the rest. It really is that simple.

Why trust us?

First and foremost we are independent. We’re not owned by any other company, and nor are we affiliated with a particular manufacturer. That’s not to say just ANY product can be installed via Thermly – we have an approved range of proven products from established manufacturers with a track record in the sector.

We will never try to sell you a particular product – we therefore try and ensure that a range of products will be available through our installer network for you to choose from.

We comprehensively vet our installer network to ensure they are competent and capable, and only appoint installers who are local to our customer base. Then, in the hopefully unlikely event you need something addressing, they’re able to respond quickly. We also undertake independent quality assurance checking of their work on a regular basis – so we know they continue to do a good job.

We've been involved in the energy and finance sectors, and in retrofitting buildings, for over 20 years. That makes us well-placed to help you find the best heat pump for your home, and identifying the right support should you need it. We’re not here to simply sell you something you don’t need - you come first, your needs come first and if something is not suitable for you, we’ll tell you.
